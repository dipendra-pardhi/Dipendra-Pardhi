# 🌟 About Me

💡 I am a passionate and detail-oriented **Data Analyst** with strong expertise in transforming raw and complex datasets into meaningful business insights using modern analytics tools and technologies.

📊 My experience includes building **interactive dashboards**, performing **data cleaning**, conducting **exploratory data analysis (EDA)**, creating **forecasting models**, and analyzing customer and business performance data to support data-driven decision making.

📈 I specialize in working with tools like **Power BI, SQL, Python, Excel, PostgreSQL, Tableau, Pandas, NumPy, and DAX** to solve real-world business problems through analytics and visualization.

🚀 I have developed multiple end-to-end analytics projects involving:

- KPI Tracking Dashboards
- Revenue Analysis
- Customer Segmentation
- RFM & CLV Analysis
- Churn Analysis
- Sales Forecasting
- Trend Analysis
- Business Intelligence Reporting
- Data Visualization
- Performance Monitoring

🎯 As a Business Analytics student, I continuously focus on improving my analytical thinking, business understanding, and technical skills to build impactful solutions that combine data, storytelling, and strategic insights.
